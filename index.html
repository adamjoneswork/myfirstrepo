About Me

I'm Adam, a passionate UX/UI designer with a focus on creating exceptional user experiences. With a degree in Drama, Theatre, and Performance, I bring a unique blend of creativity and technical expertise to my design work.

Trained and certified in UX/UI at the University of Birmingham, I've developed a deep understanding of the design process. From user research to wireframing and prototyping, I excel in crafting intuitive and user-centered designs. Driven by my love for technology and aesthetics, I constantly seek innovative ways to enhance usability and delight users.

Connect with me to collaborate or inquire further. Let's craft exceptional user experiences together.
